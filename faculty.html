CKRT draws on more than 45 expert preceptors across Yale and UConn Health, spanning the kidney, urologic, and hematologic disciplines and research areas from genomics and epidemiology to bioengineering, computational science, and clinical trials.

Every appointed trainee works with a primary research mentor — each of whom has completed mentor training and been approved by the Administrative Committee — together with a secondary mentor at the partnering institution and a broader mentoring team focused on research success and career development.
